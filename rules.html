Basic Rules of Volleyball

Home Rules Quiz

1. Team Composition

Each team consists of 6 players on the court.

2. Scoring

A point is scored on every serve for the winning team of the rally (Rally Scoring).

3. Rotation

Teams rotate clockwise every time they win the serve from the opponent.

4. Hits

Each team is allowed a maximum of three successive contacts of the ball.
